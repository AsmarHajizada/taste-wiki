# Inbox

Dump anything here. Don't worry about format. Just write.

Examples of what to drop in:
- "watched [x] loved it / hated it / complicated"
- "reading [x] halfway through, feeling [y]"
- "quote from [x]: ..."
- "thought: ..."
- "song that's been on repeat: [x]"

When you're ready, go to your AI agent and say: **"process my inbox"**
